About Us

Agua , Paani , Water, or H2O - it goes by many names , but is recognised worldwide as "the Miracle Liquid Liberating Life". Most of the evolution theories state that life started with water. Science tells us of life first begining in water , in the form of single-cell organisms, and History gives us accounts of the earliest civilizations established on the banks of water bodies.There is absoultely no other Liquid substance which has earned the reputation of being so abundantly available and at the same time vital for energy creation and the sustenance of Life. Even when we look at other planets far away from earth, we are always looking for water as a sign of 'possible life'. About 71% of our own planet- Earth is covered with water , that gives it the name 'The Blue Planet' ! Even the human body is similarly built , which is made up of 60% Water. As the 'Fuel for Life' , water is vital for all living things to flourish , the lack of which can cause severe health issues and threatens life as we know it. Water really is the biggest ELIXIR of life ! So let's revere the most valueble resource on the planet .

SIRI is a brand of packaged drinking water , by Mountain Aquatech pvt.ltd , whose philosophies embodies the 'Life Giving Spirit of Water' in the most Innovative and Gracious approach to perceiving Bottled Water. 'SIRI' derived from the Kannada word meaning 'Treasure' , signifies one of the core values of the brand which is ' to treasure or cherish Water '. Be it through ensuring quality standards of pure & safe drinking water enriching & revitalising it's consumers ,or by celebrating the life spirit of water by giving back to society though our social initiative bottles , or by helping businesses scale up achieving organic growth though our custom bottling line. SIRI has set out to make a name for itself as The Brand of Bottled Water that is not just safe and pure but also makes a statement.
